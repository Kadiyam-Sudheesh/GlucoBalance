About GlucoBalance

GlucoBalance is an empathetic digital health assistant designed to help diabetic and pre-diabetic users understand their data, build healthy habits, and stay confident.

Safety & Ethics

  • Does not replace professional medical advice
  • Never claims to cure diabetes
  • Focuses on gentle, habit-based improvements
  • Encourages regular doctor consultations

Important

This platform is not a replacement for professional medical advice. For medication changes or medical concerns, please consult your doctor.
